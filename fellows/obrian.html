Obrian Ndhlovu is a doctoral candidate in the School of Economics at the University of Cape Town (UCT), a Junior Research Fellow under the Environmental Policy Research Unit (UCT) and a Lecturer of Economics at the University of Zambia. His research interests are in agriculture, natural resource, poverty and development economics. His Thesis is looking at climate change adaptation among resource constrained smallholder farmers, with a focus on Zambia.
