Robertson Khataza is currently a lecturer at the Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources (LUANAR), Malawi. He received his PhD in Agricultural and Resource Economics from the University of Western Australia (UWA) and a Master of Development and Resource Economics from the Norwegian University of Life Sciences (NMBU). His research interests include the economics of conservation and environmental sustainability, agri-food systems, household welfare, and ICT applications in agriculture.
