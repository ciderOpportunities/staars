Musa Hasen Ahmed holds a Ph.D. in Economics from Maastricht University (UNU-MERIT), the Netherlands, and an MSc in Agricultural Economics from Haramaya University, Ethiopia. His research interests span across several vital areas including smallholder agriculture, climate economics, agricultural innovations, welfare dynamics, human capital investment, and public policy analysis. With over 10 years of experience, Musa has contributed significantly through teaching at higher education institutions and engaging in research and consultancy projects for organizations like the World Food Program and the World Bank.
